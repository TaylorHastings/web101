Vintage Vinyl

1954 introduced the very first Newport Jazz festival. It took the popular cool jazz style
and and the new hard bob jazz to the forefront of the jazz scene. Other styles exploded
like model, bossa nova out of Brazil, free jazz and finally to west coast jazz; represented
here by Chet Baker.The Everly Bothers from Kentucky were country-influenced rock and roll
singers, known for steel-string guitar playing and close harmony singing.

Listen to Dave Brubeck Quartet: Take Five
  1. Recorded in 1959.
  2. Written in e flat minor and 5/4 time. Hence the name of the song.
  3. Unlikely one-hit wonder and the best-selling jazz single of all time.

Listen to Chet Baker: Time After Time
  1. West coast jazz.
  2. Release in 1956
  3. After rehabilitated verbratto.

Listen to The Everly Brothers: Til I Kissed You
  1. Released in 1959.
  2. Chet Atkins, famous for creating the foundational 'Nashville Sound' played guitar.
  3. Peaked on the Billboard charts at #4.
